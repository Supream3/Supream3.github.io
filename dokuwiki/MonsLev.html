monster_levels

This is a list of the statistics of the monsters as they occur naturally - if you try to check their skills by using a geo (even a shallow one) their levels will be different from the levels listed here. Also remember to check the list of exceptions at the bottom of the page, just-in-case you happen to be trying to use of them in a geo or at a mod event.

Important Note: Monster's do not have a “level” stat. What we've always referred to as their “level” is really just their To-Hit rating +1. For example, the Dragon Overlord has a to-hit rating of 90, which means his “level” is 91, which is also the highest he will train your dodge level.

Where are they? We've added a simple key to help you figure out which realms monsters are found in (see below) - where there are no locations listed that monster doesn't occur naturally in the game and will only be available as a Mod summon, in Geos, or in the Tower of Infinity.

NR = Normal RealmD = Dungeons
SR = Spirit RealmL = Labyrinth
ROTD = Realm of the DeadDL = Deep Labyrinth
DR = Dragon RealmBM = Labyrinth Basement

Although most of the monsters listed here can be found anywhere in the realm, those marked as Boss or Boss Minions in their notes can only be found at one or two locations which will be on the various realm maps.
